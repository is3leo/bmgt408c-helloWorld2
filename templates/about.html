DCC

Standing for Design Cultures and Creativity, this living and learning program introduces ideas of blending innovation and creativity. A 19 credit fulfillment, DCC requires an introductory class, a capstone preparation class, and a capstone class requirement. The other 6 credits can be fulfilled during the underclassmen years with the ability to choose between a list of course offerings that pertain to their focus of study or creativity. The final 3 credits must be completed in an additional way.

  • HDCC105 : 3 cr
  • HDCC106 : 3 cr
  • HDCC208 : 3 cr
  • HDCC201 : 1 cr
  • HDCC209 : 3 cr
